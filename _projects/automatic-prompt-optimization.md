This thesis project studied prompt optimization for medical language models by treating prompt tuning as a continuous optimization problem over text embeddings.

Highlights:

- Studied gradient-based prompt optimization for medical language models.
- Introduced a Bayesian reliability estimator for candidate prompts.
- Improved factual precision by 12% on internal medical QA benchmarks.
