I am an M.S. student in Applied Artificial Intelligence at **Stevens Institute of Technology**. I work with [**Prof. Hao Wang**](https://www.stevens.edu/profile/hwang9) in the [Intelligent Systems Lab](https://intellisys.haow.us/) on LLM forgetting, continual learning, federated learning, and related language model problems. I also work with [**Prof. Feng Liu**](https://www.stevens.edu/profile/fliu22) in the Brain Imaging and Graph Learning Lab on medical AI, knowledge graphs, and seizure modeling.

My research interests include **LLM reasoning and planning**, **LLM forgetting**, **continual learning**, **federated learning**, **reinforcement learning**, **tool-using agents**, **knowledge graph reasoning**, and **AI for health**. I am especially interested in systems that combine principled learning algorithms with reliable deployment and evaluation in real-world settings.

Before joining Stevens, I worked with [**Prof. Wensheng Gan**](https://wsgan001.github.io/) at Pazhou Lab, Jinan University, on prompt optimization for medical language models, and interned at **Siemens** on data processing, validation, and KPI analysis.

I am actively looking for Ph.D. opportunities and research collaborations in reliable language model systems, agent learning, federated/continual learning, and medical AI.
