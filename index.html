About me

I am an M.S. student in Applied Artificial Intelligence at Stevens Institute of Technology. I am currently working with Prof. Hao Wang in the Intelligent Systems Lab on language model reasoning and agent systems, and with Prof. Feng Liu in the Brain Imaging and Graph Learning Lab on medical AI, knowledge graphs, and seizure modeling.

My research interests lie in LLM reasoning and planning, reinforcement learning, tool-using agents, knowledge graph reasoning, and AI for health. I am particularly interested in building systems that combine principled learning algorithms with reliable deployment and evaluation in real-world settings.

Before that, I worked with Prof. Wensheng Gan at Pazhou Lab, Jinan University, on prompt optimization for medical language models, and interned at Siemens on data processing, validation, and KPI analysis.
